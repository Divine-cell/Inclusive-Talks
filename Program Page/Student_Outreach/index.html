Student
Outreach Program

We support Nigerian students by providing essential study materials and provisions. Join us in breaking barriers to education and building a brighter future. Empowering Dreams, One Student at a Time!

How Does It
Work?

Inclusive Talks Student Outreach Program is targeted at bridding the gap of financial inclusion among Nigerian students. Through sponsorships and donations, we are able to cater to the miscellaneous needs of Nigerian students, making the road to youth empowerment less trifling.

How To
Participate

The top participants of the Inclusive Talks Student Outreach Program will receive essential study materials and provisions to support their academic journey. You can be one of these special winners by completing engaging tasks and activities designed for you.

Support This
Initiative

Join us in making a difference! This initiative relies on the generosity of individuals and organizations to empower Nigerian university students.

Ways To support:

  • Donate
  • Sponsor
  • Spread The Word
