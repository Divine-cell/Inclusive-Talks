About Us

We believe diversity is the one true thing we all have in common. We are dedicated to promoting inclusion for all in the youth space.

About Us

How It Started

In April 2022, we started out as a blog, Hae’s Thoughts, aimed at creating awareness of issues in the youth space. Later on, we re-launched as Inclusive Talks, a blog focused on fostering diversity and inclusion among young people.

Where We Are

With a global reach of over 2000 individuals across our various platforms, we leverage our blog and social networks to educate the public on topics related to diversity and inclusion. We proudly serve as a leading voice for the youth. To date, our impact has extended to over 2500 individuals worldwid

Our Goals

Inclusive Talks aims to create a personalized platform for youths to voice their diversity and inclusion concerns. Our goal is to foster a safe, supportive space for open expression and contribute to an ongoing dialogue, empowering the youth to share their experiences.
