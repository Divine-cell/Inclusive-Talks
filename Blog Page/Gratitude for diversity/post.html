Gratitude For Diversity

Celebrating 1 Year of Promoting Diversity!

5 min read. June 15 2023

Photo Credit: Pixels

Imagine a beautiful garden with a variety of colorful flowers, each with its unique shape, size, color, and fragrance. As you walk through the garden, you notice that each flower is distinct. Some flowers are tall and graceful, while others are small and delicate. Some are vibrant and bold, while others are soft and subtle. Together, they create a resplendent tapestry of colors and textures that is both awe-inspiring and uplifting.

As you take in the beauty of the garden, you realize that the diversity of the flowers is what makes it so captivating. Without this diversity, the garden would be less beautiful and less interesting. Each flower has its unique qualities that contribute to the beauty of the garden.

Just like the flowers in the garden, people are diverse in their own unique ways. We each have our talents, personalities, and experiences that make us who we are. When we come together, we create a resplendent tapestry of cultures, beliefs, and perspectives that make our world a more beautiful place.

If diversity didn’t exist, our world would be a dull and boring place. Imagine a world where everyone looked the same, spoke the same language, and had the same beliefs, values, and ideas. There would be no variety or uniqueness. Everything would be monotonous and predictable. If life were predictable, it wouldn’t be living.

Without diversity, our world would be unable to adapt to change. Different cultures, beliefs, and ways of thinking provide valuable insights that help us to navigate through the challenges of the world. It is our different ideas and perspectives that help us solve problems. This would be different if we all thought the same way.

There would be no opportunity for growth without diversity as everyone would have the same skills and knowledge. There would be no exchange of ideas. Creativity would be a myth. There would be no inspiration or motivation to try new things. Everything would be stagnant.

Diversity is like a treasure trove waiting to be harnessed. To fully enjoy its benefits, we need to embrace the different perspectives, experiences, values, and talents that it offers. The first step to embracing diversity is appreciating it.

We already talked about the blessings that diversity brings. It is the identity of our world. The least we could do is be grateful for it. How do we express our gratitude for diversity?

The best way to express gratitude of any kind is to acknowledge it. Acknowledge that we are all unique. No two people are exactly alike. When we interact with people, we must bear this in mind. By being conscious of our differences, inclusion follows.

Inclusion is the conscious effort to create a welcoming and respectful environment where individuals from diverse backgrounds feel valued and supported. It is the only true way to show that diversity is acknowledged and appreciated.
Inclusion goes beyond simply tolerating differences. It involves actively promoting a culture of openness, empathy, and respect. It involves actively working to ensure that all individuals, regardless of their race, ethnicity, gender, religion, age, or ability, have equal access to opportunities and are treated fairly and respectfully. This is the goal of Inclusive Talks carved by Hilda Enyioko. We hope to foster a generation grateful for diversity and ready to embrace it
